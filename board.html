President: Caitlin Chou

She is the most experienced out of all the members in robotics. She has been competing in the VEX competitions since she was in 8th grade when she went to VEX Worlds. She has an excellent understanding of the different committees that our club has, and facilitates cooperation between committees with elegant ease. Being able to effectively communicate her ideas, we are able to come up with great designs. We wouldn't be where we are without her amazing personality and talents!

Vice President:
Herb Hagemann

He is the most resourceful board member out of all the members in robotics. His outgoing personality makes him a wonderful Vice President. In hard times, Herb is able to lift everyone's spirits. We have been able to come so far due to his wonderful support. In previous years, he has utilized diagrams to come up with designs for the robot.

Co-Director of Public Relations & Treasury:
Bhavesh Patel

He has been in the club since his freshmen year. He truly is able to keep this club running with his novel methods of fundraising. With a recent idea, he was able to around $2000 from just donations. Without him, there's no way that our club would have the proper funding necessary to run. Bhavesh has an outgoing and funny personality. He leads the PR Committee and is a key member of 11040A and 11040B.

Co-Director of Public Relations & Treasury:
Amanda Chiang

She too has been in this club for a long time. She and Bhavesh Patel organize vital fundraising events and competitions. She is currently the head of the Game committee, and she is a member of the PR committee. She is a trustworthy member of board, and her devotion toward Robotics Club shines throughout club meetings and competitions. Although she is the head of the Game committee, she is also a prominent member of 11040B, engineering alongside them. We really apprecaite all the help she provides.

Director of Engineering:
Richard Tsai

His knowledge of everything related to Robotics makes him the first stop for all Robotics members. From coding to website designing to engineering a robot, he knows it all! He's always able to make everyone laugh and always cares about the members. His great communication skills allow him to be clear and concise with directions so that team members understand the process of engineering the robots. He's currently the head of the Engineering Committee and offers advice to both robots, not only his.

Director of Programming:
Christopher Lo

Christopher Lo, a junior, and the leader of programming for both robots. His expertise in programming makes him the first stop for all programmers. He is always able to lend a helping hand and always is willing to teach anyone about programming He is more than capable of programming in RobotC and is currently the head of the Programming committee. As the Director of Programming, he is required to constantly communicate with both the Programming and Engineering Committee. His tactical nature helps the team greatly. He definitely does do this, and he has served on the Programming Committee since the day he stepped into Robotics Club and he has been engineering for a long time also.

Director of Engineering Notebook:
Kanishk Kumar

Kanishk Kumar, a junior, and the Director of Engineering Notebook. He, along with all other board members, has been in the club since freshmen year and is the leader of the Engineering Notebook Committee. His position requires him to write and keep the Engineering Notebook for both robots. This notebook is similar to a daily diary for the robot. He tracks and records the progress in 11040A and 11040B every week while helping the others build. He is very succesfull in being able to tackle this work-heavy task. In past years, he has had experience recording, organizing, and planning out the Engineering Notebook.

Lead Public Outreach Officer / Local Outreach Manager:
Chloe Chu

Chloe Chu, a sophomore, the youngest on board, and the public outreach officer. Her charismatic personality makes her a friend to all. She is an important member of the PR Committee and the Game Committee. She helps Bhavesh and Amanda organize events and competitions. She is always very helpful in making sure the members are well treated. No one has ever been hungry at competitions! She is also a great help when engineering.
