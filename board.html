Board

All members of the Herricks Robotics Club Board are passionate and determined individuals who will go to any extreme for just robotics.

President: Caitlin Chou

Caitlin Chou, a senior, and the leader of 11040B. She is possibly the most experienced out of all the members in robotics. She has been competing in VEX competitions since she was in 8th grade when, she went to Worlds. She is currently part of two robotics teams, 11040B, and her church team. She has been a part of this club for a long time; lead the engineering committee and the programming committee. She has an excellent understanding of the different committees that our club has, and facilitates cooperation between committees with elegant ease.




Vice President: Herb Hagemann

Herb Hagemann, a senior, and a valued member of 11040A. He is possibly the most resourceful board member out of all the members in robotics. His outgoing personality makes him a wonderful Vice President, and the Director of Engineering is truly lucky to have him on his robot's team. Furthermore, he led the game and PR committee prior to getting this position. He and Caitlin work amazingly together to come up with ideas to make our club better. In previous years, he has utilized diagrams to come up with designs for the robot.




Co Public Relations Officer / Co Director of Treasury: Bhavesh Patel

Bhavesh Patel, a junior, the Co Public Relations Officer and the Co-Director of Treasury. He has been in the club since his freshmen year. He and Amanda Chiang organize vital fundraising events and competitions. Without him, our club would most likely not have the proper funding necessary to run the club. He leads the PR Committee and is a key member of 11040A. Last year, he was in the PR Committee and a Public Relations Officer.




Co Public Relations Officer / Co Director of Treasury: Amanda Chiang

Amanda Chiang, a senior, the Co Public Relations Officer and the Co-Director of Treasury. She too has been in this club for a long time. She is currently the head of the Game committee, and she is a member of the PR committee. She is the most trustworthy person on board, and her devotion toward Robotics Club really shone through during the competition. Although she is the head of the Game committee, she is also a prominent member of 110440B, and engineers along with them. She has also been a Public Relations Officer.




Director of Engineering Notebook: Kanishk Kumar

Kanishk Kumar, a junior, and the Director of Engineering Notebook. He, like all other board members, has been in the club since freshmen year and is the leader of the Engineering Notebook Committee. His position requires him to write and keep the Engineering Notebook for both robots. This notebook is sort of like a journal for the robot. He tracks and records the progress in 11040A while helping Richard and the others build. In past years, he has had experience recording, organizing, and planning out the Engineering Notebook. He is a very organized individual.




Director of Engineering: Richard Tsai

Richard Tsai, a senior, and the team leader of 11040A. His knowledge of everything related to Robotics makes him the first stop for all Robotics members. From coding to website designing to engineering a robot, he knows it all! His great communication skills allow him to be clear and concise with directions so that team members understand what to change on the robots. He is currently the head of the Engineering Committee and offers advice to both robots, not only his.




Director of Programming: Christopher Lo

Christopher Lo, a junior, and the leader of programming in both robots. He spends more of his time with 11040B and is considered a member of 11040B. His expertise in programming makes him the first stop for all programmers. He is more than capable of programming in RobotC and is currently the head of the Programming committee. As the Director of Programming, he is required to constantly communicate with both the Programming and Engineering Committee. He definitely does do this, and he has served on the Programming Committee since the day he stepped into Robotics Club and he has been engineering for a long time also.




Lead Public Outreach Officer / Local Outreach Manager: Chloe Chu

Chloe Chu, a sophomore, the youngest on board, and the public outreach officer. Her charismatic personality makes her a friend to all. She is an important member of the PR Committee and the Game Committee. She helps Bhavesh and Amanda organize events and competitions. She is also a great help when engineering. She makes the commercials for the school to get more people to sign up, she also has organized the donating forms.
