Our club now

The Herricks Robotics club, located at Herricks High School in New Hyde Park, NY, is centered around engaging students in an all-around STEM experience.

We participate in both the FIRST Tech Challenge and VEX competitions against competing schools. While competing, we aim to provide an educational experience in programming, engineering, and collaboration.

Currently, for the In the Zone VEX competition, we have taken home the Judges award, Excellence award (which advanced us to NY states), and the Amaze award at Southern NY States!
