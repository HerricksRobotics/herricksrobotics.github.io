General

Please Donate! Even a few dollars would go a long way. We are still accepting donations, please fill out the form and hand it to any robotics club member.

Last competition, one of our robots - 11040B almost made it to finals, but was sadly eliminated in a nail-bitting semifinals. Though, this robot did receive the Judges Award, both robots are aiming for states this year. Many members of Robotics agree that the first competition's outcome was the first and last time they would end up as semifinalists and quarterfinalists. They vowed to make their robots the best, so that it would guarentee them spots in states, nationals, and even worlds!

This club is one of the largest clubs in all of Herricks High School. Herricks Robotics Club meets on Tuesdays from 3 to 4:30 in the tech room. Our door is always open to new members, whether they are freshmen, or seniors. When competitions start approaching, we go into overdrive, more meetings, and each meeting longer than the last. We consider ourselves as very organized individuals who do not know the definition of pulling an all-nighter. We would love any new members, just come and speak to any robotics member on how to sign up, and any questions you have regarding Robotics Club.
