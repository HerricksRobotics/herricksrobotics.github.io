General

Amazing job at the competition at LFNY, New York to those who attended, and great work to everyone. Our hard work paid off today in our recognition through the Excellence Award, which qualifies us for States and National Championships. We showed everyone that through teamwork, perseverance, and talent, Herricks Robotics can succeed. Both of our robots were allies in the finals, but unfortunately experienced technical difficulties in the semifinals once again. Congrats to team 11040B on the amazing robot, outstanding engineering notebook, and enthusiasm that resulted in the Excellence Award! Great job to everyone with high spirit, great scouting, and the passion for robotics that will carry both teams forward in our next competitions. Our team as a whole was truly one of the best there, and with a drive to win, we will get far this season!

In the first competition, that took place at Carle Place, Long Island, team 11040B received the Judges Award. The Judges Award is presented to a team that the Judges determine is deserving of special recognition. One of the key reasons for getting this award was our amazing sportsmanship and chanting, carrying our team, a nd powering our drivers to the semifinals. At semifinals, due to technical reasons, our robot could not advance to finals. The team members of 11040B were contempt with this award but knew that another award should be won. In other words, they knew that this was only the stepping stone for where their robot could go.

This club is one of the largest clubs in all of Herricks High School. Herricks Robotics Club meets on Tuesdays from 3 to 4:30 in the tech room. Our door is always open to new members, whether they are freshmen, or seniors. When competitions start approaching, we go into overdrive, more meetings, and each meeting longer than the last. We consider ourselves as very organized individuals who do not know the definition of pulling an all-nighter. We would love any new members, just come and speak to any robotics member on how to sign up, and any questions you have regarding Robotics Club.
