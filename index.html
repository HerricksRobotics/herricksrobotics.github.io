Our Robots

11040A

Headed by Director of Engineering, Richard Tsai, this robot uses a mobile goal lift.

11040B

Led by president Caitlin Chou, this robot has five different parts that work together to meet the object of the game.

Elevator Lift

The elevator lift is the base of the chain bar lift. It moves the chain bar lift up and down.

Goliath Intake

The goliath intake grabs yellow cones by rolling down on the yellow cone, lifting it up, and rolling the opposite way to release the cone.

Chain Bar Lift

The chain bar lift lifts up cones. The goliath intake is attached to it. The chain bar lift is attached to the elevator lift.

Chassis

The chassis is the base of the robot. It has the wheels of the robot.

Mobile Goal Lift

The mobile goal lift picks up mobile goals to score points in the zone.
