    Here is our beautiful executive board for the 2017-2018 school year. From left to right, Richard Tsai, Chris Lo, Bhavesh Patel, Aman Sawhney, Caitlin Chou, Herbert Hagemann, Kanishk Kumar, and Chloe Chu.

    Co-Presidents

    Our amazing co-presidents Caitlin Chou and Aman Sawhney. They oversee the building, programming, and other preparation for our competitions. They also help introduce new members and make them always feel welcome.

    • Contact them

    Vice-President

    Herbert Hagemann is our Vice-President. His roles include taking over president role if neither are present and being far more involved in individual groups. He assists the head of each group to make the design, build, and programming go as smoothly as possible.

    • Contact him

    Director of Public Relations and Finance

    Our PR, Bhavesh Patel. He is in charge of the recruitment of new members and spreading of awareness about our club. He creates advertisements, in the form of television and paper ads, to increase our membership.

    • Contact Him

    Director of Engineering

    Our director of engineering, Richard Tsai. He leads the creation of the design that the Engineering Notebook and him agree on. He instructs the builders and works with them to complete the robot before the deadline.

    • Contact Him

    Director of Programming

    Our director of programming, Christopher Lo. He leads the programming team to success, giving advice and answering questions. When he is not doing that, he is right there with the programmers, coding just like them.

    • Contact him

    Engineering Notebook

    The true designer, Kanishk Kumar. Although he is far less involved in building, he does the majority of the design. He calculates proper measurements and what tools you need and draws a detailed view on what he wants to the robot to look like so the building team can carry out his dream.
